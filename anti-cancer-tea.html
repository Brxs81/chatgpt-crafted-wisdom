Tea Formula (per liter)

  • 1 tsp Green Tea
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Root (or ½ tsp powder)
  • 1 tsp Ginger Root
  • 1 tsp Dandelion Root
  • 1 tsp Red Clover
  • 1 tsp Milk Thistle (crushed seed)
  • 1 tsp Holy Basil
  • Optional: 1 tsp Reishi Mushroom (chopped)
  • Pinch of black pepper (for turmeric absorption)

Preparation: Simmer roots (turmeric, ginger, dandelion, reishi, milk thistle) for 15–20 mins. Add the rest, steep for 10 mins. Strain and enjoy warm.

Functional Herb Categories

  • Antioxidants: Green Tea, Turmeric, Holy Basil – protect cells from oxidative damage.
  • Anti-inflammatories: Turmeric, Ginger, Red Clover – reduce chronic inflammation linked to cancer.
  • Liver Support: Dandelion Root, Milk Thistle – assist with detox and hormonal balance.
  • Immune Modulators: Holy Basil, Reishi Mushroom – enhance immune defense and repair.
  • Hormonal Support: Red Clover – provides isoflavones; use with care in hormone-sensitive cases.

This tea is designed for general wellness and prevention — not as a standalone treatment.
