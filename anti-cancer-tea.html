Anti-Cancer Support & Prevention Tea Blend

This herbal tea blend was created with ChatGPT’s assistance, blending ancient herbal wisdom with modern AI-powered research. It is designed to support natural cancer prevention and general wellness.

Ingredients

  • Green Tea
  • Turmeric
  • Ginger
  • Dandelion Root
  • Red Clover
  • Milk Thistle
  • Holy Basil (optional)
  • Reishi Mushroom (optional)

Functional Herb Categories

  • Antioxidants: Green Tea, Turmeric – Protect cells from oxidative stress.
  • Anti-inflammatories: Ginger, Turmeric, Red Clover – Help reduce chronic inflammation.
  • Immune Modulators: Holy Basil, Reishi – Support immune resilience.
  • Detoxifiers: Milk Thistle, Dandelion Root – Assist liver detox pathways.

Preparation

Simmer roots (Ginger, Dandelion) for 15–20 minutes. Then add remaining herbs and steep for another 10 minutes. Strain and enjoy warm.

Side Effects & Precautions

  • Consult a healthcare provider before use, especially if on medications or undergoing cancer treatment.
  • Some herbs may interact with blood thinners or hormone-sensitive conditions.
  • Avoid if pregnant or breastfeeding unless approved by a professional.
